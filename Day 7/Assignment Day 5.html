Shopping list

Clothing

  1. Graphic or statement top
  2. High-waisted jeans or wide-leg pants
  3. Lightweight jacket or overshirt

Shoes

  1. Clean sneakers
  2. Ankle boots

Accessories

  1. Fun earrings
  2. Simple necklace or layered chains
  3. Small crossbody bag or mini backpack
  4. Hair accessory

Optional Extras (for uniqueness)

  1. Temporary glitter gel or shimmer makeup
  2. Nail polish
  3. Cute pins or patches for jacket/bag

Makeup

  1. Moisturizer
  2. Sunscreen
  3. Foundation
  4. Kajal
  5. Bindi

Steps list

Start with the base

  • Put on the statement top and high-waisted jeans/pants.
  • Make sure it feels comfy enough to move and dance in.

Add a layer

  • Wear the jacket or overshirt to give the outfit shape and style.
  • Roll sleeves slightly for a relaxed look.

Accessorize

  • Add earrings and a necklace
  • Put on your crossbody bag.

Final touches

  • Style hair with clips or a headband.
  • Add light shimmer makeup or lip gloss.
